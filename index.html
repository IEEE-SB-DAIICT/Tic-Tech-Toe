i.Fest

i.Fest, is the largest technical fest of Gujarat.

Over 20 events are organised, where established personalities are invited from every industry to interact and provide endeavouring insights to young minds. The focus is on bridging the gap between industries and budding engineers and the aim has been successfully accomplished for over a decade now.

IEEE Women in Engineering Affinity Group (WIE)

IEEE WIE is the largest international professional group dedicated to provide the young women with a platform and inspire them to come up with excellence in the field of Engineering.

Through Tic Tech Toe, WIE DA-IICT aims to promote women participation and encourage them to hone their technical skills.
